About College

The District of Koderma in Jharkhand is surrounded by many historical and religious places like wise Telaiya Dam, Micca Rajrappa Mandir, Bhadrakali Mandir ltkhori, Dhanbad Maithan dam Jharkhand Teachers Training College, Jhumri Telaiya, Koderma was established in the year of 2006 at the district headquarter of Koderma for imparting high standard of education to fulfill the desire of people of entire Hazaribag, region. Jharkhand Teachers Training College is well equiped for the teaching of traditional an modern Education course of B.Ed. standard prescribed by the Vinoba Bhave University, Hazaribag. This Mahavidyalaya is affiliated by the Vinoba Bhave University, Hazaribag.
